Classic Macaroni Salad

Home

Description

This creamy macaroni salad always gets lots of compliments. It's an easy recipe to make with macaroni pasta, celery, onion, bell pepper, carrot, and pimentos and has a pleasing tangy dressing that everyone seems to love!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Condiments(Mayonnaise and mustard)
  • Sugar and Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni in salted water.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise,mustard,seasonings,vineger and sugar to taste.
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator.
