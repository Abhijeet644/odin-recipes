Tuscan Chicken Soup

Home

Description

This Tuscan chicken soup features a boneless chicken breast in creamy broth with sundried tomatoes and spinach. It's especially comforting on blustery winter days.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1/2 small onion,diced
  • 2 garlic cloves,minced
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 2(8.25 ounce) containers chicken bone broth
  • 3/4 teaspoon Tuscan seasoning
  • 1 skinless,boneless chicken breast,cut into pieces
  • 1 handful roughly chopped spinach
  • 2 tablespoons sun dried tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup high protein paste
  • 2 tablespoons finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Add oil to saucepan over medium heat. Once oil is hot,add onion,and saute until translucent,about 5 minutes.
  2. Add garlic,saute until fragrant,about 1 min.
  3. Pour in chicken bone broth and add Tuscan seasoning,chicken pieces,sun-dried tomatoes,and torn spinach and stir.
  4. Add the pasta and simmer until pasta is tender.
  5. Sprinkle in Parmesan;stir to combine.
  6. Stir in heavy cream and serve immediately.
